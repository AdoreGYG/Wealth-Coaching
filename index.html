Fixed Deposit Rates vs Bank Lending Rates (2024-2025)

Key Market Insights

  • Interest Rate Spread

    Banks maintain a significant spread of 1.81% between lending rates (avg 4.94%) and fixed deposit rates (avg 3.13%).

  • FD Rate Trends

    Fixed deposit rates declined to 2.65% in 2024 from previous highs, with promotional rates reaching 3.90%.

  • Lending Rates

    Prime lending rate remains at 5.062% despite OPR cut to 2.75% in July 2025.

  • Central Bank Policy

    Bank Negara Malaysia reduced OPR to 2.75% in July 2025, first cut in 5 years to stimulate economic growth.

Data Sources: Bank Negara Malaysia, Trading Economics, RinggitPlus, CEIC Data (July 2025)
