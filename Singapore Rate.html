Singapore Interest Rate Comparison

Fixed Deposit Rates vs Bank Lending Rates (2024-2025)

Analysis by Katherine Goh Financial Insights

Key Insights

  • Significant Rate Spread - Banks earn an average spread of 3.24% between lending rates (5.59%) and fixed deposit rates (2.35%)
  • Fixed Deposit Rates Declined - Dropped ~1.15% since 2024 peak due to global monetary policy shifts
  • Lending Rates Remain Elevated - Average prime rate at 5.59% despite FD rate cuts, maintaining bank profitability
  • FD vs Lending Correlation - Fixed deposit rates follow global rate trends while lending rates are more influenced by domestic credit demand and risk factors
  • Historical Spread Range - The spread has ranged from 2.5% to 4% over the past decade, currently near the upper end at 3.24%
